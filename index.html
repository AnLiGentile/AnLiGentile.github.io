My Research Activity is principally focused on studying methods and techniques for semantic annotating semi-structured and unstructured content.
My main Research Areas are Information Extraction (IE), Natural Language Processing (NLP) and Semantic Web. From December 2015 to February 2017 I was a postdoctoral research scientist at the Univerity of Mannheim on the Data Search for Data Mining DS4DM project.
From May 2010 to November 2015 I was a post-doctoral research associate at the University of Sheffield in the OAK research group.
I obtained my doctoral degree with a thesis on Named Entity Disambiguation at the University of Bari, Italy in 2010.
